EARL SDN Enabled Measurement for All

EARL

EARL is an EPSRC funded project spearheaded by Queen Mary University of London and the University of Cambridge. The goal for the EARL project is to pioneer SDN enabled measurement-based network management to enhance the Internet infrastructure. More information can be found here.

About the project

EARL is supported by EPSRC grant EP/P025374/1. The project partners are Cisco Systems UK, CORSA Technology, Energy Sciences Network, Geant (UK), London Internet Exchange Ltd. and Cambridge Cybercrime Centre

The project is undertaken as a collaboration between Queen Mary University of London and the University of Cambridge and runs from 1st January 2018 to 31st December 2020.
